# Worked Example

## Input Route

Route R1 from `research-starter`: digital-government platform rollout and firm green patenting.

## Design Brief Excerpt

Research question: Did city digital-government platform rollout coincide with changes in firm green patenting?

Scope:

- Unit: firm-year.
- Setting: firms linkable to cities with rollout timing.
- Time: years covered by patent panel and rollout list.

Core constructs:

- Outcome: firm green patent count or log count, pending data check.
- Exposure: city platform rollout year, pending policy-list verification.
- Comparison: not-yet-treated or never-treated cities, pending coverage check.

First analysis plan:

- Build a feasibility table before any model.
- Required checks: city linkage missing rate, rollout-year distribution, pre/post coverage, comparison-unit availability.

Stop reason:

- The author must decide whether a causal route is substantively credible or whether the study should become descriptive.
